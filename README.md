# 🧟 Retro Survival

**Play free in your browser — no install, no ads, no energy bars:**

## ▶ [kla-apisak.github.io/retro-survival](https://kla-apisak.github.io/retro-survival/)

Endless-horde pixel survival: one survivor, auto-firing weapons, evolutions,
8 chapters with unique bosses (each with strengths and weaknesses worth
discussing...), and the Endless Abyss.

📲 On your phone: open the link, then "Add to Home Screen" for the full-screen
offline app.

💬 **Community**: the Discussions tab here will host *prompt slots* — submit
ideas for new music and visual designs, vote, and get credited in-game when
your prompt wins. First slot opens soon.

*This repository hosts the game builds. Strategy is for players to discover —
no spoilers in here.* 🕵️
